ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Muhammad Adam Fahmi bin Fazail. I was born in 2022, and my family largely consists of nine people. I've always enjoyed participating in artistic and visual activities since I was a child. Since middle school, when I was 14 years old,  I started getting more involved in the creative industry, I've wanted to learn more about creative skills to make it as versatile as possible. My strength in this creative area includes Photography, Videography, Photo and Video Editig. I also manage to master softwares such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Photoshop. I also like to make graphic design but there is much more things that I have to learn and improve and I willing to learn.
